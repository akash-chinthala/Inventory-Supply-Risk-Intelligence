# Inventory & Supplier Risk Analysis

## 🧠 Business Context
Supply chain inefficiencies lead to stockouts and delayed deliveries, affecting operations and customer satisfaction.

## 🎯 Objective
- Monitor stock levels
- Identify risky suppliers
- Reduce delivery delays

## 🔍 Approach
Analyzed delivery timelines and stock metrics to classify risks.

## 💡 Key Insights
- Top 20% suppliers contribute ~60% delays
- Low-stock items frequently correlate with delayed deliveries

## 🚀 Business Impact
- Improved supplier evaluation
- Reduced stock shortages
- Better planning decisions
